What to Look For When Buying an Agri-Business

Processing Equipment
Age, capacity, maintenance records, automation level. Replacement costs for abattoir, milling, or bottling lines are significant.
Licenses & Compliance
Food safety (HACCP, FSSC 22000), abattoir permits, veterinary approvals, environmental compliance. These take time to obtain.
Supply Contracts
Raw material sourcing agreements, farmer contracts, offtake agreements with retailers and wholesalers. Stability matters.
Cold Chain & Storage
Refrigeration capacity, cold rooms, blast freezers, temperature monitoring. Critical for meat, dairy, and fresh produce.
Throughput & Capacity
Daily/weekly processing volumes, utilisation rate, peak vs off-peak. Room for growth is a key valuation driver.
Workforce & Skills
Skilled operators, veterinary staff, quality controllers. Seasonal vs permanent staffing, training, and labour compliance.
