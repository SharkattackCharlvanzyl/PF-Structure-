What to Look For When Buying a Healthcare Business

Patient Base & Records
Active patient count, new patient flow, retention. Data compliance (POPIA/GDPR).
Professional Licenses
Practice licenses, CQC/HPCSA registration. Transfer processes vary by country.
Equipment & Compliance
Medical equipment age, calibration records, infection control compliance.
Regulatory Environment
Healthcare regulation differs by country. CQC (UK), HPCSA (ZA), Cantonal (CH).
Staff & Practitioners
Registered practitioners, locum dependency, employment contracts.
NHS/Insurance Contracts
Government health contracts, medical aid panels, fee schedules.
