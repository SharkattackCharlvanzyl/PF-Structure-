Payment FAQ

Common questions about payments, billing, and refunds.

What currencies do you accept?
All prices in USD. Your bank converts to local currency. PayPal handles conversion automatically. Bank transfers accepted in USD, EUR, GBP, and ZAR.
Is my card information secure?
Cards processed by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1). South African EFT by PayFast. We never store full card numbers — only tokenized references with last 4 digits.
How do refunds work?
Ad rejections: Full refund if you don't resubmit. Listing cancellations: Prepaid balance usable within 12 months. Payment errors: Refunded within 7 business days. Email [email protected].
What happens if payment fails?
You get an email. Service pauses (not deleted). 7 days to update payment and retry. After 7 days, listing is suspended. Reactivate anytime with successful payment.
How does auto-top-up work?
When ad wallet drops below $10, we charge your default card for your chosen amount ($50/$100/$200). Ads never pause. Disable anytime in wallet settings.
Do you offer discounts?
No. Our listing fees are already the lowest in the industry — Private Sellers: $7.50/listing/month (1–10 listings), Agency: $3.00/listing/month (11–2,000 listings), and Enterprise: $2.00/listing/month (2,000+ listings). Every listing gets the full feature set (30 photos, video, analytics, 185-country exposure) at a flat rate with no hidden charges. We keep pricing simple and transparent — the price you see is the price you pay.
