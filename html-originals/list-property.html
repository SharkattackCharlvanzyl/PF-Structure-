How Auto-Sync Works — Step by Step

1

🔗 Connect Your System

During sign-up, choose "Auto-Sync" and select your current listing platform, CRM, or website. If your system isn't listed, simply enter your website URL.

PropertyFinder connects to your existing platform via secure API integration, XML/JSON feed import, or — with your explicit permission — by logging into your system with the username and password you provide. We can sync from virtually any property website or CRM.
2

🔐 Grant Secure Access

You provide your login credentials or API key. Your credentials are encrypted with bank-level AES-256 encryption and stored in a secure vault. PropertyFinder staff never see your password.

We only need read-only access — we never modify, delete, or change anything on your existing system. We simply read your listings and clone them to PropertyFinder. You can revoke access at any time from your dashboard.
3

🤖 We Clone Everything Automatically

Our system logs into your platform, reads all your active listings, and imports everything — photos, descriptions, pricing, property details, floor plans, videos, and contact info.

What gets imported: Property title, description, price, address, photos (up to 30), video, floor plans, property type, bedrooms, bathrooms, size, land area, features list, agent name, phone, email — everything that's on your current listing.
4

👀 Preview & Approve

Before anything goes live, you review every imported listing on your PropertyFinder dashboard. Each listing shows a full preview exactly as buyers will see it.

You can edit any detail, add or remove photos, adjust pricing, or update descriptions. Nothing is published until you give explicit approval. See the Approval Process below for the full flow.
5

✅ One-Click Approval & Go Live

Once you're happy, click "Approve & Publish" — individually or in bulk. Your listings go live across 185 countries instantly.

You'll receive a confirmation email for every listing published, with a direct link to view it live. Bulk approval is available — approve all listings at once or select specific ones.
6

🔄 Daily Auto-Sync Keeps Everything Fresh

After the initial import, PropertyFinder checks your source system daily. New listings are added, sold/removed listings are archived, and price or detail changes sync automatically.

You never upload again. Update your listing on your original system and PropertyFinder mirrors the change within 24 hours. Changed listings go through the same preview process — you can choose to auto-approve updates or require manual approval for each change.

Payment FAQ

Common questions about listing, payments, and billing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, instant EFT (South Africa), bank transfer (SWIFT/SEPA), and selected local payment methods depending on your country. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment gateway.

Can I pay in my local currency?

Prices are displayed in USD but you can pay in your local currency. Your bank or payment provider will convert at the prevailing exchange rate. We're working on adding native currency pricing for major markets (ZAR, GBP, EUR, AED, CHF, SGD).

Is there a free trial or free listing option?

We don't offer free listings, but at $7.50/month for private sellers (or as low as $2/listing for enterprise), PropertyFinder is the most affordable international property platform available. Every listing gets the same 30 photos, video, portal walkthrough, analytics, and 185-country exposure. You can cancel anytime with no penalties. Creating an account is always free.

How do I cancel a listing?

Log into your dashboard → My Listings → select the listing → click "Cancel Listing". The listing stays active until the end of your current billing cycle, then it's removed. No cancellation fees. Your price tier adjusts automatically as your listing count changes.

Do you offer refunds?

Listing fees are generally non-refundable once the listing is published. If you experience a technical issue that prevents your listing from being displayed, contact support within 7 days for a review. Duplicate payments are refunded automatically.

Do you offer agency or bulk discounts?

Volume discounts are automatic. With 11+ active listings your price drops to $3/listing, and at 2,000+ listings it drops to $2/listing. All features remain identical — 30 photos, video, portal walkthrough, analytics, WhatsApp, featured badge, and 185-country exposure at every tier. No need to negotiate or apply — it happens automatically as your listing count grows.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and our payment gateway is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. We never store your full card details on our servers. Payments are processed by Stripe / PayFast / PayPal (depending on region).

What happens if my payment fails?

If a payment fails, your listing remains active for 3 days while we retry. You'll receive an email notification with a link to update your payment method. If payment is not resolved within 7 days, the listing is paused (not deleted) until payment is made.
