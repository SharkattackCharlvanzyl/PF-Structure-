Business Listing FAQ

Common questions about listing a business for sale on PropertyFinder.

Is this the same system as property listings?
Yes — business listings and property listings share the same platform, same pricing ($7.50 / $3.00 / $2.00 per listing), same payment system, same analytics, same notifications, and same 185-country buyer exposure. The only difference is the listing form — it has specialised fields for business financials (turnover, EBITDA, employees, etc.) instead of property fields (bedrooms, bathrooms, etc.).
Can I auto-sync from my existing CRM?
Yes. We support direct API sync with BizBuySell, BusinessesForSale.com, Tupelo CRM, DealRelations, Deal Studio, Business Brokers CRM, and many more. We can also scrape your own broker website with your permission. If your system has an export function or API, we can connect it — usually within 48 hours.
How do I protect confidential financials?
You can set any financial field to "Available after NDA" in your dashboard. Buyers must digitally sign a non-disclosure agreement before they can view sensitive data like revenue, profit, customer lists, or exact address. The NDA workflow is built into PropertyFinder — no external tools needed.
What business sectors do you support?
All major sectors: Restaurants & Food, Retail & Commerce, Technology, Professional Services, Hospitality & Tourism, Franchises, Healthcare & Medical, Automotive, Agriculture & Farming, and Manufacturing. Each sector has specialised listing fields and badges. If your sector isn't listed, choose "Other" and describe it in the form.
Do you charge commission on sales?
No. PropertyFinder charges zero commission on any sale. You pay only the flat listing fee ($7.50, $3.00, or $2.00 per listing per month depending on volume). Your buyer-broker relationship and fee structure remain entirely your own.
Can I list a business for auction?
Yes. Any business listing can also be listed for online auction. Set your reserve price, choose the auction duration (3, 5, 7, or 14 days), and let verified buyers bid competitively. Same auction system as property — real-time bidding, anti-sniping, auto-bid, and instant notifications.
What payment methods do you accept?
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Instant EFT (South Africa via PayFast), bank transfer (SWIFT/SEPA), Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely via Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1). See our Payment System page for details.
How quickly does my listing go live?
After payment, our team reviews your listing within 24 hours. Once approved, it goes live instantly across 185 countries. Auto-synced listings from CRMs can be bulk-approved — all your listings can be live within minutes of approval.
