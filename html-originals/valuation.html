FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ProVal a certified professional valuation? ▼
ProVal is an AI-powered market analysis report, not a registered valuer's certificate. It provides an accurate market value estimate for informational and negotiation purposes. For official bank or legal valuations, a registered property valuer may be required in some jurisdictions.
How accurate is the AI valuation? ▼
ProVal targets a ±7% accuracy range based on available comparable sales data. Accuracy depends on the availability of recent comparable sales in your area. In areas with high transaction volumes, accuracy is typically higher.
Can I get a valuation for any country? ▼
Yes — ProVal is available for residential properties in all 185 countries on the PropertyFinder platform. The report adapts to local market conditions, currencies and available data sources.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept credit/debit card and PayPal. All payments are secured and processed at $124 USD (converted to your local currency at checkout where applicable).
How quickly do I receive my report? ▼
Your ProVal report will be delivered within 60 minutes of payment being confirmed. A PDF copy will be sent to your email address as soon as the report is ready. In most cases delivery is sooner — we will notify you by email the moment it is complete.
Can I get a valuation for a property that's not listed on PropertyFinder? ▼
Yes — you can enter any residential property address manually. The ProVal tool will run the AI analysis based on the address and property details you provide, even if the property is not currently listed on our platform.
Is there a refund policy? ▼
Once your report has been generated and delivered, refunds are not available as the service has been fully rendered. Please review the free example report before purchasing to ensure ProVal meets your needs.
