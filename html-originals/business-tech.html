What to Look For When Buying a Technology Services Business

Client Contracts & Retainers
Recurring monthly retainers vs one-off project work. Retainer-heavy businesses have more predictable revenue and higher valuations.
Team & Key-Person Risk
Developers, designers, and account managers. Can the business run without the founder? Staff retention is critical.
Tools & Systems
Project management, CRM, hosting accounts, software licenses. Well-documented processes reduce transition risk significantly.
Portfolio & Reputation
Client testimonials, case studies, Google reviews, industry awards. A strong portfolio commands higher prices and wins new business.
Revenue per Client
Client concentration risk — if one client is 30%+ of revenue, that's a risk. Diversified client base is preferred.
IP & Digital Assets
Proprietary tools, templates, frameworks, domain names, hosting infrastructure. These add tangible value beyond the client list.
