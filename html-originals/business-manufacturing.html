What to Look For When Buying a Manufacturing Business

Equipment & Machinery
Age, condition, replacement cost, CNC and automation level.
Facility & Lease
Factory size, layout, expansion potential. Freehold vs leasehold affects valuation.
Quality Certifications
ISO 9001, IATF 16949, HACCP/BRC. These take years to obtain.
Order Book & Contracts
Current backlog, long-term supply agreements, customer concentration.
Workforce & Skills
Skilled operators, apprenticeship programs, shift patterns.
Compliance & Environmental
Environmental permits, waste handling, HSE record.
