Accepted File Formats

PNG (preferred — lossless, transparency)
JPG/JPEG (photos, no transparency)
GIF (animated, max 3 loops, 15 sec)
SVG (vector, scales perfectly)
WebP (modern, smallest file size)

📏

Minimum Requirements

Resolution: Exact pixel dimensions (or @2x retina)
DPI: 72 minimum (144 for @2x)
Colour: RGB colour space (not CMYK)
Quality: No blur, pixelation, or artifacts
File size: See table per placement

⚠️

What Gets Rejected

Wrong dimensions or file format
Blurry, pixelated, or low-quality images
Misleading or deceptive content
Inappropriate or offensive material
Auto-play audio
Mimics PropertyFinder UI
File size exceeds maximum

Advertising FAQ

Common questions about self-service advertising.

Do I need to design my own ad?
Yes — you create your own ad creative to our specifications. This gives you full control over your brand and messaging. Use any design tool (Canva, Photoshop, Figma) and export in PNG, JPG, GIF, SVG, or WebP at the exact pixel dimensions for your placement.
What file format do you recommend?
PNG is recommended — lossless quality with transparency support. Use JPG for photo-heavy ads, GIF for simple animations (max 15 seconds, 3 loops), and SVG for vector graphics. Always supply @2x resolution for retina displays.
👁️ Want to see exactly how your ad will look? View our Visual Ad Preview Guide — with laptop and mobile mockups for every ad type, plus full CPC pricing details and payment system explained.
How long does approval take?
Standard: up to 24 hours. Professional tier: same-day. Premium/Enterprise: instant. Every ad is reviewed by our team at [email protected]. You'll receive an email when approved or if changes are needed.
What if my ad is rejected?
You'll get an email explaining exactly what to fix (wrong size, blurry, etc.). Resubmit at no extra cost. Payment is held until approval. Full refund if you choose not to continue.
Can I change my ad after it's live?
Yes — upload a new creative from your dashboard anytime. The replacement goes through review. Your current ad runs until the new one is approved — no downtime.
How does geographic targeting work?
Your ad shows only to users browsing listings in your selected region. A Cape Town attorney's ad only appears on Cape Town listings. You can target by country, region, or specific city.
What if I don't have design skills?
Free tools like Canva make it easy — search for "web banner" templates at the right size, add your logo and text, export as PNG. Takes 10 minutes.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No contracts, no lock-in. Your $50 minimum deposit is your only commitment. Use it over a week or a year — your choice. Cancel anytime.
