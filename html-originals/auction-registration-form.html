💰 Bidder Commitment & Deposit Requirement

To ensure fairness, transparency, and serious participation for all parties in our international property auctions, we require a fully refundable commitment deposit from potential bidders:

  • Deposit USD 1,000 (or equivalent in an agreed currency) into your attorney's trust account or a compliant escrow/attorney holding account in your jurisdiction.
  • Your attorney or authorised financial representative must provide PropertyFinder with an official letter confirming that the funds have been received and are held in trust.
  • If you are the successful bidder, this USD 1,000 will be credited toward the final purchase price (including any required deposit or balance payment).
  • The deposit is fully refundable if you do not win the auction, or if the transaction does not proceed due to circumstances beyond your control. Refunds are processed promptly — typically within 5–7 business days.

This is a standard, widely accepted practice in property auctions globally. It protects sellers, maintains auction integrity, and allows genuine bidders to participate confidently with minimal risk.
